Online Learning Platforms in Physical Education

Source 1

URL Blended Learning in Physical Education: A Systematic Review
Author Wang, C., Omar Dev, R. D., Soh, K. G., Mohd Nasirudddin, N. J., Yuan, Y., & Ji, X.
Abstract This article, published in Frontiers in Public Health, presents the results of a study on the application of blended learning, which includes traditional in-person instruction combined with synchronous or asynchronous online learning within the setting of a physical education classroom. In total, 22 scientific papers have been considered in the Web of Science. This systematic analysis reveals the trends related to the types of participants involved, the online resources used, theoretical approaches, and ways of evaluating blended learning. This source is relevant because it provides a broad, evidence-based overview of how online learning platforms are being integrated into PE instruction and the outcomes that follow.

Source 2

URL The Impact of Digital Tools and Online Learning Platforms on Higher Education Learning Outcomes
Author Rafiq, S., Iqbal, S., & Afzal, A.
Abstract This article is about a study that explores the impact of technology and online learning platforms on learning outcomes in higher education by focusing on the engagement, motivation, and academic performance of university students. After surveying 350 students enrolled in some universities, a significant increase in positive academic performance and critical thinking skills with the help of online learning platforms was recorded. This study proves beneficial to my research because it shows how well virtual learning platforms, no matter the resource, perform in the classroom setting.
